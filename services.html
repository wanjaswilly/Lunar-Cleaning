Our Cleaning Services

Residential Cleaning

Offering a thorough, top-to-bottom cleaning service tailored to homes and apartments. This service includes dusting, mopping, vacuuming, sanitizing, and more.

Key Features:
  • Dusting all surfaces
  • Vacuuming carpets and rugs
  • Sweeping and mopping floors
  • Cleaning and sanitizing kitchens and bathrooms
  • Trash removal and recycling
Ideal For: Busy families, professionals, and homeowners who need regular or deep cleaning.

Commercial Cleaning

Providing professional cleaning for office spaces, retail stores, and other commercial properties. A clean workplace promotes productivity and a positive atmosphere

Key Features:
  • Cleaning and sanitizing restrooms, breakrooms, and common areas
  • Dusting and wiping down workstations, furniture, and electronics
  • Emptying trash and recycling bins
  • Floor care (vacuuming, mopping, etc.)
  • Restocking supplies (paper towels, toilet paper, soap)
Ideal For: Offices, retail businesses, corporate spaces.

Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning

A detailed cleaning service designed for clients moving in or out of a property. This ensures that the space is immaculate for the next tenant or homeowner.

Key Features:
  • Complete dusting and vacuuming of the entire property
  • Cleaning and sanitizing all rooms, including the kitchen and bathrooms
  • Cleaning inside and outside of all appliances
  • Window cleaning (interior)
  • Wiping down baseboards, doors, and light fixtures
Ideal For: Tenants, landlords, property managers, and real estate agents.

Deep Cleaning

A comprehensive cleaning service focused on areas that are often missed during routine cleaning. Ideal for homes and businesses that need a seasonal or one-time deep clean.

Key Features:
  • Cleaning under and behind furniture
  • Deep scrubbing of floors, tiles, and grout
  • Cleaning inside ovens, refrigerators, and other appliances
  • Washing walls, doors, and windows
  • Detailed dusting of hard-to-reach areas
Ideal For: Homes or offices that haven’t been cleaned in a while or those preparing for a special event.

Specialty Services

A range of specialized cleaning services for unique needs.

Key Features:
  • Window Cleaning: Sparkling, streak-free windows (inside and out)
  • Carpet Cleaning: Professional carpet cleaning for residential and commercial spaces
  • Post-Construction Cleaning: Removing dust, debris, and construction residue from newly renovated or built properties
  • Eco-Friendly Cleaning: Using green cleaning products for an environmentally friendly clean.
Clients with specific cleaning needs like allergy sufferers, businesses post-renovation, or homes with pets.

Custom Cleaning Packages

Tailored cleaning packages that fit the specific needs and schedule of your clients.

Key Features:
  • Flexible scheduling (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly)
  • Personalized cleaning checklists
  • Available for both residential and commercial clients
Ideal For: Clients looking for ongoing maintenance with customized service options.
